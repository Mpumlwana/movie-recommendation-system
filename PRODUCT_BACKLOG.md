## Prioritization Justification

Must-have stories directly support core user actions such as login, searching, rating, and receiving recommendations. These align with stakeholder success metrics related to usability, performance, and user satisfaction.

Should-have stories improve system administration and scalability, addressing operational and business needs.

Could-have stories enhance analytics and monitoring, providing additional value but not required for the MVP.

High-value stories are prioritized to maximize user satisfaction and ensure early delivery of essential system functionality.
